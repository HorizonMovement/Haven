Part of the Haven Ecosystem – Tools for Trust, Transparency, and Resilience

Pulse

Pulse is a transparent crypto trading assistant designed to protect users from market manipulation, scams, and fake utility tokens. With simulation and data integration already complete, Pulse is evolving into a fully live trading platform guided by user-first ethics.

Ember

Ember is the trust layer of the ecosystem, offering token reputation scoring, wallet behavior tracking, and the Pulse Verified badge—helping users distinguish safe projects from deceptive ones in real-time.

Echo

Echo is an upcoming ethical AI advisor that provides education, real-time risk warnings, and emotional resilience tools for Web3 traders. Transparency is nothing without understanding—and Echo ensures users never trade in the dark.
